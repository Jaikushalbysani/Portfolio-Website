Webpage on "MARVEL UNIVERSE"

         The design of my Marvel Cinematic Universe website is a dynamic portal that immerses visitors in the rich tapestry of Marvel's cinematic legacy. Through a thoughtfully structured layout, users can easily navigate a comprehensive collection of information, spanning from the earliest Marvel films to the latest blockbusters. Engaging visuals, including posters and stills, capture the essence of each movie, while concise yet informative summaries provide a glimpse into the Marvel saga. The website's sleek interface showcases a blend of classic and modern aesthetics, paying homage to Marvel's evolution over time. With an intuitive search feature and interactive timeline, fans can delve deep into the history of their favorite superheroes and gain a deeper understanding of the cinematic universe that has captivated audiences worldwide.

UI / UX Design of "TOWARDS MARS"

         Crafting the Mars Ticket Booking app's design, I aimed for a sleek fusion of functionality and futuristic aesthetics. The interface showcases Mars as a backdrop, guiding users seamlessly through date selection, spacecraft preferences, and accommodations. With clear typography and subtle animations, the user experience remains engaging and intuitive. Secure payment options ensure smooth transactions, and a dynamic confirmation screen adds an element of excitement with a launch countdown. This design not only facilitates Mars travel bookings but also immerses users in an unforgettable visual journey.

UI / UX Design of "What's In Mess"

          The design of my college daily food menu app embraces simplicity and practicality. With a user-centered approach, the interface offers easy navigation, displaying the day's meals in a clean and organized layout. Vibrant visuals of each dish are accompanied by clear descriptions, making it effortless for students to plan their meals. Intuitive filters and search options enable quick access to dietary preferences and special offerings. The app's color palette exudes a welcoming and energetic vibe, while its user-friendly design ensures seamless interaction, allowing students to effortlessly stay informed about their daily dining options.
